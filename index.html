Nominitter

This is the home of Nominitter, a redonkulous twitter game where you tweet the username of the person who best meets the criteria of the current round. For example:

Round 1: Most likely person you know to get arrested. Nominate your pick by tweeting @ them with hashtag #nominitter. The results will be posted here.
